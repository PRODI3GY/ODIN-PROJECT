RAMEN RECIPE

Description

This is an easily prepared meal that requires minimal effort and time to prepare and is popular among college students especially for that very reason.

Ingredients

  • A packet of ramen noodles
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cabbage
  • Lemon
  • Chillies

Steps

  1. Boil the ramen noodles for 10 mins in hot water
  2. Chop onions,tomatoes,cabbage and chillies
  3. Wash onions in salty water
  4. Mix the cabbage,onions,tomatoes,chillies
  5. Squeeze lemon on the mixture then serve the ramen when hot
