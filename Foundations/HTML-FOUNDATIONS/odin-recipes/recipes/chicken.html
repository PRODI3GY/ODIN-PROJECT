Chicken Recipe

Decription

Ingredients

  • Chicken Meat
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken in water for 20 mins
  2. Fry onions in cooking oil till they turn to golden brown
  3. Add Tomatoes and garlic and cover for 7 minutes
  4. Add chicken meat and let it cook for 30 minutes while stirring occasionally
