A BEEF RECIPE

Description

This is beef recipe which is mostly served with other dishes and is recommended when one wants a heavy meal, it is best served hot and is recommended to be eaten as early dinner or lunch

INGREDIENTS

  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

STEPS

  1. Fry onions till they turn golden brown
  2. Add Garlic and Beef and let it cook for 20 mins while occasionally stiring
  3. Add ginger and wait for five minutes then serve
