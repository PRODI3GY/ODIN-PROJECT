A Rice and Meat Recipe

Description

This is a rice and meat recipe, which is best served hot and recommended to be eating during dinner or lunch time. Some people also refer to it as a snack and eat it moments after breakfast or before a main course

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic

STEPS

  1. Boil rice in water for 10 minutes
  2. Thinly slice tomatoes and onions
  3. Crush the garlic in motor and pestle
  4. Fry onions in oil then add garlic and tomatoes once onions turn golden brown
  5. Add meat and cook for 20 mins, serve rice and meat when hot
